Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Amus College School is dedicated to nurturing learners academically, spiritually, morally, and in talent.

  • Motto: Let there be light.
  • Location: Bukedea District, Eastern Uganda, one kilometer off Mbale-Soroti highway on Amus Sapir Hill.
  • Admissions: Ongoing for Senior one to Senior six. P.O. Box number 2058.

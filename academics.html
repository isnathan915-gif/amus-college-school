Curriculum

Our Academic Offerings

Senior One to Senior Two

Students build core understanding in key subjects while developing discipline, values, and learning confidence.

Core Learning Areas

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education
  • Creative Arts & Physical Education

Enrichment Areas

  • Storytelling & Reading Clubs
  • Music, Dance, and Drama
  • Basic ICT and digital learning
  • Local Language and Culture

Character & Values

  • Discipline & leadership foundations
  • Peer mentorship program
  • Community awareness
  • Environmental consciousness

Senior Three to Senior Four

Learners deepen understanding and prepare for UCE with structured coursework and continuous assessment.

Core Academics

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science

Humanities

  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education
  • Reading Comprehension

Practical Subjects

  • Creative Arts
  • Physical Education
  • Computer Studies

Senior Five to Senior Six

Advanced level studies preparing students for university entry and career readiness through focused subject combinations and leadership development.

Advanced Subject Focus

  • Sciences combination
  • Humanities combination
  • Business combination

Talent & Leadership

  • Student leadership roles
  • Community service projects
  • Mentorship programmes

University & Career Readiness

  • University application guidance
  • Career counselling
  • UACE examination preparation

Co-Curricular Activities

We believe education extends beyond the classroom. Every pupil is encouraged to participate in at least one co-curricular activity.

Sports

Football, netball, basketball, volleyball, athletics, and swimming.

Drama & Arts

Annual drama festival, fine art exhibitions, and creative writing competitions.

Debate Club

Inter-school and national-level debates — developing critical thinking and public speaking.

Music & Choir

School choir, band, and traditional music ensemble perform at national events.

ICT Club

Coding, robotics, and digital design workshops led by industry mentors.

Environment Club

Tree planting, recycling drives, and sustainability education projects.

Location, Goals & Admissions

Location

Amus College School is located in Bukedea District, Eastern Uganda. One kilometer off Mbale-Soroti highway on Amus Sapir Hill.

Goals

The college is a universal institution interested in providing a bright future in both education and talent of the community.
